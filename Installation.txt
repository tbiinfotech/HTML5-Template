
Instructions to creating a new Template:
-----------------------------------------
--Create a .html in the /html5 directory
--Use the following template (also a sample in /html5/index.html):

<title><!-- Title of your template, note this appears in the document title prefixed with "HTML5" --></title>
<style>/** any custom styles live here **/</style>
<article><!-- any markup here --></article>
<script>
// your JavaScript
</script>

--Any additional JavaScript libraries should be stored in the /js directory, assets, such as video and audio live in the /assets directory.
